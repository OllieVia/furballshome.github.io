Grooming

Our Grooming Services

Full Groom

Our Full Groom consists of brush out, ear cleaning, nail trim, bubble bath with shampoo and conditioner for the fresh fur, fluff dry and full body hair cut and styling of your choice.

Small breeds eg. chihuahua, yorkie:€35-€50

Medium breeds eg. king charles: €40-€55

Large breeds eg. german shepard: €50-€60

Partial Groom

For our partial grooms we provide hair and nail trimming, brush out, ear cleaning, bubble bath, and fluff dry. We also tidy up the face and paws.

Small breeds eg. chihuahua, yorkie: €20-€30

Medium breeds eg. king charles: €25-€40

Large breeds eg. german shepard: €35-€50

Eye and Ear Cleaning

We also provide eye and ear cleaning for the fur babies that need it.

Eye Cleaning: €5

Ear Cleaning: €5

Eye and Ear Cleaning: €10

If the dog is being difficult: extra €10

Nail Trim

A trim for nail that have gotten too long for your doggo.

Cost: €5

If the dog is being difficult: extra €10

De-Shedding Treatment

De shedding is the physical removal of a dog’s loose undercoat which is good for your dog and their comfort. We use multiple tools and methods to de-shed your fluffy companion depending on their coat type.

Cost: €15-€25

Dematting Treatment

Dematting is the removal of tangles and knots in your pups fur.

Cost: €15-€25
