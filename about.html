About Us

Fur Balls Home is a dog training and care centre that is based in Co.Wicklow. We provide some of the best classes and lessons taught by some of the most qualified instructors around for your fur ball.

Fluffy Friends Home specialise in teaching your dog good behaviour, rehabilitation of dogs that have come from traumatic backgrounds, socialising dogs with other dogs, we also have areas for introverted dogs that may feel overwhelmed when surrounded by other dogs - we are a second home for your cuddly companion.

We also provide first class doggy day care for all dog types so that you do not need to feel guilty when you are away during the day, we will look after your dog. Our service includes picking up your dog and returning when you are home. We have a specialised spa area where dogs can be groomed, we have a heated pool on site so your dog can enjoy swimming!

We also provide a doggy hotel which is a home away from home, rest assured, your dog is safe with us while you enjoy that time away. We simply care about your dog. It shows as we are 20 years in business, and have a five star rating with 1,000 positive comments on google.
