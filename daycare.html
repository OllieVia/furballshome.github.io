Daycare Play Areas

We got three play areas for your fur baby to choose from!

The areas that we have are the outdoor area, extroverted area for those energetic wild dogs and an introverted area for the more quiet dogs that don’t do as well with other dogs

Introverted dogs areas

Our Daycare has decided to have small introverted areas for the dogs that don’t do well in a large area filled with other dog.

In this area your dog will have their own caretaker to play with during the visit.

Don’t worry we have plenty of toys for your little introvert to play with. Even though their aren’t any other dogs their, your doggo will surely not feel lonely here.

Extroverted dogs areas

Here is just the perfect place for those dogs that enjoy having friends over.

This area is huge and has tonnes of space for your fluffy companion to run around and have fun with others

Your dog is bound to make some play date friends in here!

Outdoor play area

Our Daycare provides an enormous play area for dogs to go wild and adventure.

You don’t need to worry about your dog running out as we have a tall fence surrounding the area to make sure that your dog doesn’t run away.

Either way due to the amount of fun that your dog will have we doubt they’d want to run away anyway.
